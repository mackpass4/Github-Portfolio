How do you express your creativity?

I really cherish the ability to create using a variety of different media/mediums . I believe that art is about an individuals ability to express, in whatever form that may manifest. Skill and talent may be desirable in terms of 'success', but art is more personal than any judgement another person can put upon you, therefore success is irrelevant.
